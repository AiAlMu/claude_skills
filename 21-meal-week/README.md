# Meal Week

Creates a 7-day meal plan with a complete shopping list and one meal prep tip. Simple meals, no exotic ingredients, under 30 minutes to cook.

## Install

Copy `meal-week.md` to your `.claude/commands/` folder.

## Usage

```
/meal-week [your preferences, restrictions, budget]
```

## Example

```
/meal-week No pork, trying to eat more protein, budget around $60/week for groceries, cooking for one person, not much time on weekdays
```

Claude will give you a full week of meals and a shopping list organized by aisle.
