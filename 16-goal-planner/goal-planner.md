description: Break down a big life goal into a practical weekly action plan
---

Take the following goal and turn it into a clear action plan. Include:

1. **3-month milestone** — what "real progress" looks like in 90 days
2. **Monthly targets** — month 1, 2, 3 broken down
3. **This week's actions** — 3-5 specific things to do in the next 7 days
4. **Biggest obstacles** — the top 2 things that will slow me down and how to handle them

Be realistic, not motivational. Skip the fluff.
