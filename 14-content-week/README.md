# Content Week

Generates 7 days of LinkedIn or Instagram posts for your AI/automation freelance business. Mixed content types — tips, case studies, questions, offers.

## Install

Copy `content-week.md` to your `.claude/commands/` folder.

## Usage

```
/content-week [describe your service and target audience]
```

## Example

```
/content-week I offer n8n automation services to small e-commerce businesses. I help them automate order confirmations, abandoned cart emails, and inventory alerts.
```

Batch your entire week of content in one go.
